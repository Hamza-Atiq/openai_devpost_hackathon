# Contributor guide

Read `scope.md`, `prd.md`, `spec.md`, and the assigned `checklist.md` task before editing.

- Preserve 8 teams, 2 groups, 2 venues, and 15 matches in Version 1.
- Never relax a confirmed hard constraint or bypass independent validation.
- Agents interpret and explain; deterministic services create and validate fixtures.
- Keep guest identity in secure same-origin cookies and all secrets server-side.
- Start with a focused failing test; finish with the consolidated quality gate.
- Do not conceal failures in evidence or claim completion without fresh output.
- Do not start optional/Version 1.1 work without explicit approval.
- Keep weather language as risk guidance and approval as workspace-internal.

Handoffs must list changed files, requirement IDs, tests, limitations, and deployment impact.
